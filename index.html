Welcome to the Ebola Dashboard

Mobile Collection Instructions: iPhone & Android

The Ebola Dashboard is a tool for viewing and analyzing real-time data collected in the field pertaining to Ebola deaths and suspected infection. This is a Fulcrum Gives Back initiative, which is supported as resources will allow. The system functions will change and develop as individuals in the field provide feedback. If you are in West Africa and need this collection tool, contact us at ebola@fulcrumapp.com or on twitter @fulcrumapp.

All data currently shown is entirely fictitious, for demonstration purposes only.
